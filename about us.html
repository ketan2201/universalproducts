About Us

Welcome to Universal Products – Your Trusted Spring Manufacturer Since 1987

At Universal Products, we take immense pride in our legacy of over three decades as a leading spring manufacturer. Established in 1987, our journey began with a commitment to deliver precision-engineered springs of the highest quality.
